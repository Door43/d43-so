====== 1. The Creation ======


**1. Abuuritaanki Ugu Horeeyey** 


[[:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-01.jpg?media=https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-01.jpg|{{:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-01.jpg?nolink&}}]]


This is how the beginning of everything happened. God created the universe and everything in it in six days. After God created the earth it was dark and empty, and nothing had been formed in it. But God's Spirit was there over the water.


Sidan ayeey u dhacday bilowgii inta aan Illaah (Swt) wax abuurin. Illaah(Swt) wuxu aduunka oo dhan iyo wax dul joogay lix cisho gudeheed.Marku Illaah (Swt) abuuray dhulka wuu madooba wanu madhna, waxna ma dhex oolin Laakin ruuxda Illaah (Swt) waxay dul joogtay biyah korkeeda.


[[:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-02.jpg?media=https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-02.jpg|{{:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-02.jpg?nolink&}}]]


Then God spoke and said, "Let there be light!" And there was light. God saw that the light was good and called it "day." He separated it from the darkness, which he called "night." God created the light on the first day of creation.


Kadibna Illaah (Swt) ayaa hadlay oo yiri, “ Ha ahato iftiin!” kadib iftiin ayeey ahatay. Kadib Illaah (Swt) ayaa arkay iftiinki yirina “ Waa maalin” Madaowgii ayuu ka dhexsaray maalinti yidhina waa “ habeen.” Illaah (Swt) wuxu sameyay iftiinka maalinti ugu horeysay.


[[:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-03.jpg?media=https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-03.jpg|{{:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-03.jpg?nolink&}}]]


On the second day of creation, God spoke and created the sky above the earth. He made the sky by separating the water above from the water below.


Maalintii labaad ee abuurida, Illaah (Swt) wuxu abuuray cirka oo ku saraysiyeey dhulka.Cirkana wuxuu ka socay biyaha cirka iyo kuwa dhulka.


[[:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-04.jpg?media=https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-04.jpg|{{:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-04.jpg?nolink&}}]]


On the third day, God spoke and separated the water from the dry land. He called the dry land "earth," and he called the water "seas." God saw that what he had created was good.


Maliintii saddexaad, Illaah wuxuu abuuray dhuul engegen wuxuuna kala socay biyah badaha ku jira, ilaaha biya galeen ka ah iyo webiyaadda. Wuxuu Illaah ku daboolay dhul banana, taagyo, iyo buuro doog leh, ubaaxayo, canab, iyo geedo.Saliid qunbe, tufaax, iyo midho kala duwaan oo ka baxa geedaha iyo keymo.


[[:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-05.jpg?media=https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-05.jpg|{{:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-05.jpg?nolink&}}]]


Then God said, "Let the earth produce all kinds of trees and plants." And that is what happened. God saw that what he had created was good.


Illaah (Swt) wuxu yiri, “ dhulku haa soo saaro dhir kala duwan iyo miro.” sidaas ayeey noqotay.Kadib Illaah (Swt) wuxu arkay in aay abuuristisu cajiib tahay.


[[:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-06.jpg?media=https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-06.jpg|{{:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-06.jpg?nolink&}}]]


On the fourth day of creation, God spoke and made the sun, the moon, and the stars. God made them to give light to the earth and to mark day and night, seasons and years. God saw that what he had created was good.


Maalinti afraad, Illaah (Swt) wuxuu saray wax yar oo huruuda oo ku wareegsan circa. Waxay ahayd qoraxda, Fiidki dayax cad ayaa so muqday, iyo xidigo fara badan oo cirka ku daadsan


[[:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-07.jpg?media=https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-07.jpg|{{:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-07.jpg?nolink&}}]]


On the fifth day, God spoke and made everything that swims in the water and all the birds. God saw that it was good, and he blessed them.


Maalinti shanaad, Qayloo ayaa meel kaste ka yeraysay.Muxuu Illaah abuuray maalintaas?Wuxu ummaay shimbiro ku duulaya cirka iyo malaay ku dabalanya biyaha. Dhamaan wey kala duwanayeen qaab da’ahan, qaab ahan iyo midab ahanba.Nebiri, kaluun, shimibiro buluuga, xuur, iyo qudun quto waxay ka mid ahayen inta yare ee Illaah ummaay ugu horeynti.


[[:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-08.jpg?media=https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-08.jpg|{{:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-08.jpg?nolink&}}]]


On the sixth day of creation, God said, "Let there be all kinds of land animals!" And it happened just like God said. Some were farm animals, some crawled on the ground, and some were wild. And God saw that it was good.


Maalinti lixaad, Illaah wuxuu abuuray Xayawaanka guryaa joogta ah, xayawaanaadka gurgurta, iyo xayawaanadka kale oo la arki karo.


Illaah waxa kalo uu aburay lo’da, fardaha, laxaha, eyda, iyo bisaadaha, sido kale wuxu abuuray libaxayda, shabeelka, yeeyga iyo bakayleh.


[[:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-09.jpg?media=https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-09.jpg|{{:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-09.jpg?nolink&}}]]


Then God said, "Let us make human beings in our image to be like us. They will have authority over the earth and all the animals."


Wuxuu Illaah(Swt) yiri, “ Aynu sameeyn bini aadam kana sameyno aragtidayda. Waxay noqon doonan ku wa xuma dhulka iyo xayaawanka oo dhan.”


[[:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-10.jpg?media=https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-10.jpg|{{:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-10.jpg?nolink&}}]]


So God took some dirt, formed it into a man, and breathed life into him. This man's name was Adam. God planted a garden where Adam could live, and put him there to care for it.


Markasu Illaah (Swt) dhoobada ka sameyey nin, kuna afuufay neeftisa isag. Kadib ninkina u bixiyay Aadam.Illaah (Swt) wuxu abuuray beer uu ku noolado si uu isna beerta u xanaaneyo.


[[:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-11.jpg?media=https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-11.jpg|{{:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-11.jpg?nolink&}}]]


In the middle of the garden, God planted two special trees—the tree of life and the tree of the knowledge of good and evil. God told Adam that he could eat from any tree in the garden except from the tree of the knowledge of good and evil. If he ate from this tree, he would die.


Beerta dhaxdeeda, Illaah (Swt) wuxu ku abuuray laba geed- geedka nolosha iyo ka dhimashada ee danbiga la helo.Wuxuna u sheegay inuu dhamaan dhirta oo dhan cuni karo laga reebo geedka dhimashada.Hadii u cunana uu ku dhiman doono.


[[:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-12.jpg?media=https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-12.jpg|{{:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-12.jpg?nolink&}}]]


Then God said, "It is not good for man to be alone." But none of the animals could be Adam's helper.


Kadib, Illaah(Swt) wuxu yiri, “ Ma ficnaa inu ninku kaligii noolado.” waayo xayaawanka oo dhan mid qudhi Aadam ma cawinayen.


[[:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-13.jpg?media=https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-13.jpg|{{:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-13.jpg?nolink&}}]]


So God made Adam fall into a deep sleep. Then God took one of Adam's ribs and made it into a woman and brought her to him.


Markasu Illaah(Swt) Aadam ku riday hurdo wuuna seexday. Kadib feedhiisa ayuu ka abuuray naag  una keenay iyadii isagaa.


[[:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-14.jpg?media=https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-14.jpg|{{:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-14.jpg?nolink&}}]]


When Adam saw her, he said, "At last! This one is like me! Let her be called 'Woman,' for she was made from Man." This is why a man leaves his father and mother and becomes one with his wife.


Aadam marku arkay iyaadi ayuu yiri, “ugu danbeynti! tani waa mid aniga ii eeg! kadib wuxu ugu yeeray iyada ‘ Naag,’ waayo waxa laga sameeyey nin.” sidaa ogeyd ayuu ninku uga taaga hoyadiis iyo aabihiis si uu naagtisa ula noolado.


[[:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-15.jpg?media=https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-15.jpg|{{:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-15.jpg?nolink&}}]]


God made man and woman in his own image. He blessed them and told them, “Have many children and grandchildren and fill the earth!” And God saw that everything he had made was very good, and he was very pleased with all of it. This all happened on the sixth day of creation.


Illaah(Swt) wuxuu ka buuray nin iyo naagba aragtidiisa. Wuuna barakeyey kuna yiri, “ dhalaa caruur badan iyo ubadakooda dhulkana buuxiya!” wuxuna Illaah (Swt) arkay in wax uu sameeyey yihiin wax ficaan, aadna wuu ugu farxay dhaaman. Tanina waxay dhacday maliintii lixaad ee abuurka.


[[:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-16.jpg?media=https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-16.jpg|{{:https:api.unfoldingword.org:obs:jpg:1:en:360px:obs-en-01-16.jpg?nolink&}}]]


When the seventh day came, God had finished his work. So God rested from all he had been doing. He blessed the seventh day and made it holy, because on this day he rested from his work. This is how God created the universe and everything in it.


Markay maalinti todobaad timid, Illaah (Swt) wuxu dhamaystiray shaqadisii abuurka.Wuuna ka nastay wixii uu abuuray oo dhan.Wuxuna kadhiga malinta todobaad malin barkaysan oo quduusa, waxaa yeelay wuxuu ka nasatay shaqadi abuurka. Waana sidana sida uu Illaah(Swt) u abuuray aduunka oo dhan iyo wax dhex yaala.
